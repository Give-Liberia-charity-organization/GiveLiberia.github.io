On June 27th, 2020; We visited The Life Village Orphanage Home Located in Redhill Field, Red Light

We all are always so happy and grateful to give back to the needy but today was something way special because we gave back Red oil , rice and water to 20 amazing children , their smiles and enthusiasm brought joy to all of us at Give Liberia today and that’s just the kind of motivation we need to enhance the passion that already exists in us ! It’s not about us, it’s about the people......

On June 20th, 2020; We were opportune to give back to an educational system

As a charity group that consist of only young people ,we at the Give Liberia charity group were so enthusiastic about today’s donation because we were opportune to give back to an educational system which is “The Mother Comfort Christ Miracle of Faith School System”which was officially founded in the early 2000s in efforts to educate young children in faith based learning but recently due to the Corona Virus Global Pandemic classes have ceased and teachers have gone without compensation for nearly two months. That is why we gave back 20 bags of rice to help compensate for things that are lacking in their lives during this pandemic.

On June 13th, 2020; Give Liberia charity Group were given another amazing opportunity to give back to some elderly and disabled people in the OldRoad community

Yesterday was just another productive day! We at Give Liberia charity Group were given another amazing opportunity to give back to some elderly and disabled people in the OldRoad community . They were all grateful and gave words of encouragement to us and all the amazing people who have donated towards our charity work . Some of these people walk miles to market to get food regardless of their age , health and physical situation, that’s why giving back to them means so much to Give Liberia Charity Group. It’s not about us, it’s about the people......

On June 6th, 2020; We were given the privilege to visit “The Destiny Recovery Program”

It is a drug rehabilitation and homeless center where young people that are from the slums , streets and ghettos go to learn about their purpose in life and be able to take up space in society . We at Give Liberia Charity Group felt the need to give back few bags of rice and 10,000LD as an encouragement to these people who could possibly be our future leaders tomorrow. “Dedication has no limitations “ We are dedicated to serving the people. All it takes is a helping hand.
