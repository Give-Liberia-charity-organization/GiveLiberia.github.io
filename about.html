Director of Finance

Mr. Wiah started student activism in grade school trickering him into a force for good in favor of students right and emacipation. Cherifa Abdallah

Deputy Director for Development

Miss Cherifa Abdallah is a driven humanitarian and mental health professional with a passion for rendering services to underprivileged communities in Liberia. Cherifa holds a Bachelor of Arts(BA) in Psychology with a minor in Criminal Justice from Hampton University in Virginia, USA. During her matriculation through her undergraduate studies by serving her campus community, Cherifa developed a passion for linking individuals with resources to assist them with self-reformation and betterment. Since graduating from university, Cherifa has gained experience working with individuals with diagnosed mental health illnesses as well as two Liberian based non-profit organizations. These non-profit organizations cater to women and children, providing education to the masses, and teaching young children professional trades in efforts towards personal development. Miss Cherifa Abdallah is currently pursuing a certificate in Project Management to better serve the organizations she engages with. Cherifa is excited to continue her journey of social responsibility and humanitarianism to the Liberian community through the arms of Give Liberia!
